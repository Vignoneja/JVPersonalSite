About

I am a Marine Corps Infantry Veteran (8 years of active duty & honorably discharged). After the USMC, I went to school to become a helicopter pilot, where I earned my Private and Commercial licenses. Below, is a picture of me on my very first solo flight!

While attending school for that, I was a Bartender/Manager at a resturant in Memphis, TN. (MidTown Crossing Bar & Grille).
Unfortunately, the helicopter school closed down before I could continue with my Instrument rating. That is when my wife and I decided to move to Saint Louis, MO to be closer to family and for more job opportunities.
I found myself working at the National Archives and Records Administration for the next couple of years before deciding to join up at Centriq to pursue a career in Full Stack Web development!

When I'm not working on my web development skills, I am busy staying active outdoors with my wife and daughter.
We enjoy camping, kayaking and running local 5k's (all the outdoorsy stuff really).
I am a member of a local Amvets Post and actively help them with events to raise money for struggling veterans and their families. Along with that, I am the Communications officer for the Amvets Post 6 Motorcycle Riders, where I enjoy going on rides whenever the time and weather allow and working with the riders to organize events and meetings.
