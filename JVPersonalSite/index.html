About

I am a Marine Corps Infantry Veteran (8 years in and honorably discharged). After the USMC, I went to school to become a helicopter pilot in which I gained my Private and Commercial license. Here I am on my very first Solo flight!

While going to school for that I was a Bartender/Manager at a resturant in Memphis TN. (MidTown Crossing Bar/Grille). The Helicopter school closed down before I could continue my Instrument rating. My wife and I decided to move to Saint Louis, MO to be closer to family and for better job opportunities. I found myself working at the National Archives and Records Administration for the next couple of years before deciding to join up at Centriq and pursue a career in web development.

When I'm not working on my web development skills, I am busy staying active outdoors with my wife and daughter. We enjoy camping, kayaking and running local 5k's. I am a member of my local Amvets Post and actively help them with events for raising money for struggling veterans. Along with that I am the Communications officer for the Amvets Motorcycle Riders, in which I enjoy going on rides whenever the time and weather allow. I am also an avid gamer and enjoy playing all multitudes of genres and stories of games in my downtime.
